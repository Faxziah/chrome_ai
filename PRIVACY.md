# Privacy Policy for AI Text Tools

**Last updated: November 24, 2025**

## Overview

AI Text Tools ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Data Collection

### Data We Collect

The Extension collects and stores the following data **locally on your device**:

1. **Gemini API Key**
   - Your Google Gemini API key is stored locally to authenticate requests to Google's AI service
   - The key is never transmitted to any third party

2. **Website Content**
   - Text you select on web pages for AI processing (summarization, rephrasing, translation, discussion, highlighting)
   - AI-generated responses based on your selected text
   - This data is stored in your local history and favorites

3. **User Preferences**
   - Interface language settings
   - AI model configuration
   - UI preferences

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, address)
- Browsing history or web history
- Location data
- Financial information
- Health information
- Keystroke logging or user activity tracking

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's secure storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). No data is stored on external servers controlled by the Extension developer.

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties.**

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally using Chrome's built-in secure storage
- Your API key is stored securely and never exposed to websites
- No analytics or tracking services are used

## User Control

You have full control over your data:
- **View** your history and favorites in the side panel
- **Delete** individual items or clear all history/favorites
- **Export** your data for backup
- **Remove** the extension to delete all stored data

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
